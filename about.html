About Me

John Welsh served twenty years in the United States Marine Corps. As most of his career was spent in the Counterintelligence/Human Intelligence field, John has a unique understanding of threats and vulnerabilities. Deriving his interest in information technology and cybersecurity from working with professionals in those fields, John strives to provide holistic security support to United States equities.

As a veteran of four deployments to Afghanistan and travels to other countries, John is experienced in providing support to force protection in diverse operational environments. He is often sought for guidance on blending physical security, security administration, and information security with cybersecurity.
